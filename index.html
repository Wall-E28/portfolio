Hi, I'm Riley!

Data Analyst - Python - SQL

Data Analyst with a BS in finance and mathematics from Santa Clara University and a data analytics certification for UC Berkeley Extension. I have experience in Excel, Python, and SQL with excellent communication skills gained from start-up environments. Currently transitioning from the world of sales, I am ambitious to provide workplace analysis using both programming and visualization skills. The current project I am working on uses solar radiation data from Solcast to make inferences on whether solar panels are a smart investment in different major US cities. By using my strengths of cultivating data-driven insights and a self-starter mindset, I would be a welcome addition to any organization.
